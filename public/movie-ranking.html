The Best Movies According to Zach

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Demon Slayer

One of my all-time favorites. A boy loses
everything to demons except his sister,
who is turning into one. He joins the
Demon Slayer Corps to save her and
fight back. Visually stunning and
emotionally powerful.

The Notebook

Noah, a poor man, falls in love with Allie
who comes from wealth. They are forced
to keep passion for each other aside due
to societal pressure and a difference in
the social stature of their families

Drive

One of my top 3. A stuntman getaway driver
falls for a woman stuck in a criminal world
and risks everything to protect her. Gritty,
emotional, unforgettable.
